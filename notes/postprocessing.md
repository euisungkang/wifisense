# Post-processing comparison (chunk 8)

Temporal smoothing of the per-window classifier output over the stitched continuous capture. Generated by `scripts/compare_postprocessing.py` — do not edit by hand.

- Capture windows: **71** (window_size=250, stride=25); **8** lie fully inside one activity (in-segment).
- Smoothing window: **k = 5** windows (~half a segment: 250/25 = 10 windows per segment).
- HMM transition matrix: learned from UT-HAR training labels (Laplace alpha=1.0).

## Results

Transition rate = class flips per 100 windows (lower = smoother). In-segment accuracy uses chunk 7's boundary-exclusion logic.

| Method | Per-window acc | Δ vs raw | In-segment acc | Transition rate |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| raw | 0.563 | — | 1.000 | 32.9 |
| moving_average (k=5) | 0.676 | +0.113 | 0.875 | 17.1 |
| majority_vote (k=5) | 0.620 | +0.056 | 1.000 | 20.0 |
| hmm_decode (Viterbi) | 0.718 | +0.155 | 0.875 | 10.0 |

**Best method (per-window acc, ties → smoother): `hmm`.**
